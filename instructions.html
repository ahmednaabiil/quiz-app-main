Instructions for the quiz

  • You can choose the category, difficulty and number of questions for the quiz.
  • Total duration is of 5 or 10 minutes depending on number of questions. On completion of time, the quiz is automatically submitted.
  • Each question has 4 options available. You have to choose the correct option among them.
  • There is no negative marking for wrong answers.
  • You cannot proceed to the next question without answering the current question.
  • However, you can return back to any of the previously answered questions.
  • At the end of quiz, you can view the result.
  • All the best.
